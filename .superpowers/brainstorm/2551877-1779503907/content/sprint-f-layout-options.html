Option B: Case Control Tower

Higher information density and faster repeat operations, but weaker page hierarchy and easier to overbuild in Sprint F.

My Recommendation

  • Pick A if Sprint F is about closing the primary Case -> Payload workflow.
  • It keeps Case Detail meaningful instead of collapsing too much back into the list page.

Tradeoff

  • B is denser and may feel faster for experienced operators.
  • But B increases scope because the list page starts absorbing detail-page responsibilities.
